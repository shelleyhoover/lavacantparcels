The Space for Students

Vacant Residential Space near Los Angeles Community Colleges

Historically, the city of Los Angeles has favored low-density development. As a result, 75% of L.A. residential space is zoned for single family homes.
In a city experiencing one of the worst housing crisis' in U.S. history, Los Angeles has swaths of vacant space already zoned for residences.
A 2016 study found that 1 in 5 Community College Students in L.A. are Homeless. The map shows the vacant residential space near 5 of Los Angeles' Community Colleges
This map looks at the space near these colleges.
